About Me

Hello, I am Dr. Debeswar Das, an Assistant Professor at the School of Biomedical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (Banaras Hindu University). My primary focus is on Biomechanics and its applications in health and sports.

Research Interests

  • Biomechanics and Motor Function Analysis
  • Artificial Intelligence in Neuroimaging
  • Gait Analysis and Musculoskeletal Research
  • Sports Biomechanics and Performance Optimization
  • Health Monitoring and Biomedical Devices

Current Projects

  • Development of an Artificial Intelligence System to Detect Early Shared Neural Biomarkers of ASD and ADHD from Multimodal Neuroimaging
  • Remote Health Monitoring Systems
  • Radiative Cooling Coatings for Medical Applications
  • Voice Biometric Based Security for Intellectual Property Protection
  • Photocatalytic Dye Degradation using Functionalized Nanosheets
